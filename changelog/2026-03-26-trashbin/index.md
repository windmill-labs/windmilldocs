When you delete a script, flow, app, schedule, variable, resource, or trigger, the item is now moved to the trashbin instead of being permanently removed. Admins can restore accidentally deleted items from **Workspace settings > Trashbin** within a 3-day window.

Items are automatically purged after 3 days. You can also permanently delete individual items or empty the entire trashbin.
